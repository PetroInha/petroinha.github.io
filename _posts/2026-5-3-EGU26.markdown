## A Week of Science in Vienna

EGU 2026 was a remarkable opportunity to share our work with the global geosciences
community, receive feedback from leading experts, and discover the latest advances in
CCS, subsurface modeling, and energy transition research.

We are proud of Aitiana and Eunsil for representing CURE on the international stage —
and grateful to every lab member whose contributions made this research possible.

Onward to the next conference! 🚀
