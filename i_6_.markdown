<h1>IS-QUE 2026</h1>

<h2>International Symposium on CCS & Unconventional Energy</h2>

<p>
The <strong>International Symposium on CCS and Unconventional Energy (IS-QUE)</strong> is a global forum bringing together researchers, engineers, and industry leaders working on emerging subsurface energy systems.
</p>

<p>
Key topics include <strong>Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS)</strong>, unconventional oil and gas resources, <strong>direct lithium extraction from subsurface brine</strong>, hydrogen energy systems, and geothermal energy.
</p>
