<h1>IsCUE 2026</h1>

<h2>International Symposium on CCS & Unconventional Energy</h2>

<p>
The <strong>International Symposium on CCS and Unconventional Energy (IsCUE)</strong> is an international forum bringing together leading researchers, engineers, and industry experts working on emerging subsurface energy technologies.
</p>

<p>
Key topics include <strong>Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS)</strong>, unconventional oil and gas resources, <strong>direct lithium extraction from subsurface brine</strong>, hydrogen energy systems, and geothermal energy.
</p>

# 🌍 Participating Institutions

The symposium brings together researchers from leading universities and energy companies across Asia, Europe, and the Americas.

Participating universities include:

- **Inha University (Korea)**
- **Northeast Petroleum University (China)**

- **China University of Petroleum – Beijing (China)**
