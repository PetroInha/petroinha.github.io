# International Symposium on CCS & Unconventional Energy  
### **IS-QUE 2026**

The **International Symposium on CCS and Unconventional Energy (IS-QUE)**  
is a global forum for researchers, engineers, and industry leaders working on **subsurface energy systems**.

The symposium focuses on emerging technologies including:

- Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS)
- Direct Lithium Extraction from subsurface brine
- Hydrogen energy systems
- Geothermal energy
- Unconventional oil & gas resources (oil sands / shale gas)

The event is initiated by **Dr. Hyundon Shin (PI, CURE Lab, Inha University)**.
