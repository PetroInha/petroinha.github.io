<h1 class="hero-title">IsCUE 2026</h1>

<h2>The 4th International Symposium on CCS & Unconventional Energy</h2>

<p>
The <strong>International Symposium on CCS and Unconventional Energy (IsCUE)</strong>  
is an international forum bringing together leading researchers, engineers,  
and industry experts working on emerging subsurface energy technologies.
</p>

<p>
Key topics include <strong>Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS)</strong>,  
unconventional oil and gas resources,  
<strong>direct lithium extraction from subsurface brine</strong>,  
hydrogen energy systems, and geothermal energy.
</p>

<div class="hero-meta">

<span class="hero-chip">📅 May 27–29, 2026</span>
<span class="hero-chip">📍 Northeast Petroleum University, Daqing, China</span>
<span class="hero-chip">Host: Northeast Petroleum University & Inha University</span>
